For Charities

Why Charities Should Join OneCommunity

  • Access to New Donors & Resources: Connect with local businesses and individuals eager to help.
  • Streamlined Request Management: Easily post, track, and update your donation and volunteer requests.
  • Increased Visibility: Raise your profile among local businesses and the wider community.
  • Volunteer Recruitment: Attract skilled and passionate volunteers for your projects.
  • Support & Guidance: Our team is here to help you make the most of the platform.

How to Request Donations or Volunteers

  1. Sign Up: Register your charity and create a profile.
  2. Validation: Our team will verify your details for a secure experience.
  3. Create Profile: Share your mission, focus areas, and needs.
  4. Post Requests: List specific needs for goods, services, or volunteers.
  5. Get Matched: Our platform automatically matches your requests to business offers and volunteers.
  6. Confirm & Connect: Review matches, confirm participation, and connect directly with supporters.

What OneCommunity Offers

  • Support: Personalised help from our team to maximise your impact.
  • Visibility: Your charity and requests are promoted to local businesses and volunteers.
  • Efficient Matching: Automated and human-validated matching for the best results.

FAQs for Charities

Who can register as a charity?
Any registered charity or non-profit organisation in the UK can join OneCommunity.
Is there a cost to join?
No, OneCommunity is free for charities.
How do we post a request?
Once registered and validated, you can post requests for goods, services, or volunteers from your dashboard.
How are matches made?
Our platform uses automated matching plus human review to ensure the best fit for your needs.
Can we thank our supporters?
Yes! You can send thank-you messages and updates to businesses and volunteers who help your charity.
