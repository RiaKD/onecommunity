About OneCommunity

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To empower local communities by connecting businesses, charities, and volunteers, making it easy to give, receive, and make a difference—together.

The Problem

Poverty in the UK remains a persistent and complex challenge. 21% of the population—14.3 million people—live in poverty, including 4.3 million children. Many families struggle to afford basic needs like food, heating, and clothing. The number of people experiencing destitution (lacking food, fuel, clothing, and shelter) has risen significantly in recent years.

Policy changes have had a major impact: after the 2010 Child Poverty Act, rates declined, but the 2016 Welfare Reform and Work Act reversed much of this progress. Today, child poverty rates have risen by 50% from 2016 to 2020. The effects are felt across healthcare, education, and social care, with increased demand on public services and long-term consequences for children and families.

Charities and local organisations work tirelessly, but often lack the resources or connections to meet every need. Businesses want to help, but don’t always know how or where to start. Volunteers are eager, but opportunities can be hard to find or match to their skills.

Our Solution

OneCommunity bridges the gap by using a unique approach: automated matching of business donations and volunteer offers to charity requests, combined with human validation to ensure every connection is meaningful and effective.

  • Charities post their needs and introduce themselves.
  • Businesses and individuals offer goods, services, or time.
  • Our platform matches offers to needs, then a real person reviews and confirms the match.
  • Everyone receives feedback and thanks, building trust and community spirit.
