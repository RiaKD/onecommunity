For Businesses

Why Businesses Should Join OneCommunity

  • Enhanced Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR): Make a measurable difference in your local community.
  • Community Engagement: Build relationships with local charities and residents.
  • Positive PR: Share your impact stories and boost your brand reputation.
  • Tax Benefits: Donations may be tax-deductible (consult your tax advisor for details).
  • Employee Satisfaction: Offer meaningful volunteering opportunities for your team.

How to Donate or Volunteer

  1. Sign Up: Register your business and create a profile.
  2. Validation: Our team will verify your details for a secure experience.
  3. Post Offers: List goods, services, or volunteering your business can provide.
  4. Get Matched: Our platform automatically matches your offers to charity needs.
  5. Confirm & Connect: Review matches, confirm participation, and connect directly with charities.

Types of Donations Accepted

  • Goods: Food, clothing, office supplies, equipment, and more.
  • Services: Professional expertise, logistics, marketing, mentoring, etc.
  • Financial: One-off or recurring monetary donations.
  • Volunteering: Individual or team volunteering for events, mentoring, or ongoing support.

FAQs for Businesses

What types of businesses can join?
Any business, large or small, can participate and make a difference.
Is there a cost to join?
No, joining OneCommunity is free for businesses.
How do we know our donations are making an impact?
You'll receive updates and feedback from the charities you support, and can track your impact on your dashboard.
Can we choose which charities to support?
Yes! You can browse requests and select the causes that matter most to your business.
Are donations tax-deductible?
In many cases, yes, but please consult your tax advisor for specific guidance.
