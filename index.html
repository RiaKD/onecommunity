Connecting Local Impact: Businesses, Charities, and Volunteers United

Join OneCommunity and help build a stronger, more connected local network through donations, volunteering, and collaboration.

How It Works

For Businesses

  1. Sign up and create your profile.
  2. Browse charity requests or offer donations/services.
  3. Connect with charities and track your impact.

For Charities

  1. Register your charity and introduce your mission.
  2. Post requests for goods, services, or volunteers.
  3. Connect with local businesses and volunteers.

For Volunteers

  1. Register as a volunteer and create your profile.
  2. Browse and apply for volunteering opportunities.
  3. Connect with charities and make a difference.
