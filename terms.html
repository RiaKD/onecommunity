Welcome to OneCommunity. Please read these terms carefully before using our platform.

  • Users are responsible for their own interactions, offers, and agreements made through the platform.
  • All users must comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Handling & GDPR

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • Personal data is collected only for the purpose of facilitating connections and improving our services.
  • We store your data securely and only for as long as necessary.
  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as required to provide our services or by law.

Platform Liability

OneCommunity acts as a facilitator, connecting businesses, charities, and volunteers. We do not directly participate in or guarantee the fulfillment of any donation or volunteering arrangement.

  • We are not liable for any loss, damage, or dispute arising from arrangements made through the platform.
  • Ultimate responsibility for the donation or volunteering arrangement lies with the business and charity involved.

Validation Process

To ensure trust and safety, OneCommunity validates all registered charities and businesses before they can post or accept requests.

  • Charities must provide proof of registration and relevant documentation.
  • Businesses may be asked for verification of their trading status.
  • We reserve the right to refuse or remove access to the platform for any user who does not meet our criteria.

Fundraising Regulation

OneCommunity facilitates both in-kind donations and monetary donations, as well as volunteering opportunities. We adhere to the Fundraising Regulator's Code of Fundraising Practice and are transparent about how the platform operates.

  • We do not charge fees for in-kind donations or standard platform use.
  • For monetary donations processed through our platform, we charge a 4.5% commission.
  • All fees and charges are clearly disclosed before any transaction is processed.

Charity Commission Guidelines

We follow Charity Commission guidance to ensure we only work with legitimate charities and support due diligence for donations received by charities.

  • Our validation process is designed to help charities meet their legal obligations.
  • Charities are responsible for ensuring compliance with all relevant regulations and guidance.

Fees & Commission

OneCommunity is committed to transparency in all our fee structures and charges.

  • In-Kind Donations: No fees are charged for in-kind donations (goods, services, equipment). These are processed directly between businesses and charities.
  • Monetary Donations: A 4.5% commission is charged on all monetary donations processed through our platform. This fee covers operational costs, platform maintenance, and secure payment processing.
  • Volunteering: No fees are charged for volunteer matching or coordination services.
  • Transparency: All fees and charges are clearly disclosed before any transaction is processed, and users will be informed of any changes to our fee structure in advance.

Transparency & Fees

OneCommunity is committed to transparency. If we introduce any paid or premium features, all fees and terms will be clearly communicated to users in advance.
